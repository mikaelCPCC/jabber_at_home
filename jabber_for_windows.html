Installation

  1. Download the Cisco Jabber file.

  2. Double-click the CiscoJabberSetup.msi.

  3. Read the notices and disclaimers and then click Accept and Install.

  4. Wait for Cisco Jabber to be installed.

  5. Select Launch Cisco Jabber and click Finish.

Signing In

 

  • After you have opened the Jabber application and the sign in screen appears, enter your CPCC employee username and your password, then click Sign In.

 

Signing Out

 

  • Click the Options menu in the upper right hand corner and then click Sign out or Exit.

  • You are no longer connected to Jabber.
