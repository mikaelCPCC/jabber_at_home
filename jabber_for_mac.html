Installation

  1. Download the Cisco Jabber file.


  2. Double-click the CiscoJabberMac zip file.

  3. Click the unzipped Cisco Jabber.app file and move it to your local Applications directory.

     
  4. Double click Cisco Jabber.app.

     
  5. Wait for the app to be verified.

     
  6. If this prompt appears, click Open to continue.

     
  7. Click Agree.

     

Signing In

 

  • After you have opened the Jabber application and the sign in screen appears, enter your CPCC employee username, click continue and enter your network password, then click Sign In.

 

Signing Out

  • Click Jabber from the Apple menu bar and then click Sign Out.
